Assign the SharePoint Advanced Management Administrator role to users who need to do the following tasks:

- Perform all actions available to SharePoint Administrators, including global management of SharePoint Online, support ticket handling, and service health monitoring
- View names, paths, and URLs of files, folders, libraries, documents, and lists within SharePoint sites, without accessing file or item content
- Remove permissions from files, folders, libraries, documents, and lists within SharePoint sites

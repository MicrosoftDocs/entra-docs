Assign the Teams External Collaboration Administrator role to users who need to do the following tasks:

- Manage Teams external collaboration settings that govern the organization's interactions with external users in chats, meetings, and calls.
- Configure external domains, including creating, editing, and deleting domain entries that specify how users engage with external organizations.
- Establish and manage allowlist and blocklist policies for external collaboration at both the user and group levels.
- Control which external domains and users can collaborate with the organization for secure and compliant external collaboration.
